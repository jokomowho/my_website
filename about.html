My Experience

User Experience is something I've always loved. My journey into this field started in 2006, I have been working as a User Experience Design Specialist for over 10 years now, and I have worked with multiple organizations with lots of exciting user centered driven projects. Over the years, I have lead the user exprience design of multiple projects, some of these projects span different sectors like: defense, interactive journalism, corporate hiring and talent acquisition, investment trading portal, UNICEF's RapidSMS Birth Registration; an application that shows national birth registration statistics, election monitoring tools for data capturing deployed multi-nationally, e.t.c.

I believe the user journey is always more rewarding than the destination, and problem solving should be centered around the users so I strive to look for ways to bring on the best user centered design into mobile and web applications with my craft. I love to take ownership of my work and drive engagements on my workstreams. Plus I always strive to add value to whatever team or organization I am a part of.
