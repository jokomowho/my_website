Meet Joy

Joy is a User Interface and User Experience Design Specialist. She loves the front end design of simple interfaces and cares greatly about her users. Because design shouldn't just be about slapping a bunch of pages together, Joy is always looking for ways to make her user experience skills elicit simple user interface design, that is easy to learn and use, and meets up to user requirements.

Joy resides in the United States. In her spare time she's an avid reader, foodie, listens to podcasts like TWIT, O'Reilly Design Podcast, TED Radio hour, and so on. She's also a moderate and occasional gamer, traveler and explorer.

Experience

User Experience is something I've always loved. I worked 4 years and half years as a User Experience Design Strategist in TimbaObjects Ltd., and worked on exciting projects that were user centered, some of them being RapidSMS Birth Registration, an application that shows the birth registration statistics across Nigeria, by age range and gender distribution, another being election monitoring tool used to capture data during election monitoring.

As a User Experience Design and User Interface Developer with Irodah Ltd, I worked on some interesting web application projects as well, like ARM Stocktrade, an investment trading portal, and Diakrino, a web portal.

As a Freelance Consultant, I've also worked on some interesting projects, one being an human resources recruitment tool, and another being an assessment tool to help test the level of productivity.

I believe the user journey is always more rewarding than the destination, so I strive to look for ways to bring awesomeness to the user's fingertips, and help users with my craft.
