Meet Joy

I am a User Experience Design Specialist. I care greatly about users and strive to make day to day impactful user centered driven designs. Design shouldn't just be about slapping a bunch of interfaces together, so I am always looking for ways to bring the best user experience to user's fingertips by creating aesthetically pleasing simple designs driven by user goals and buisness requirements.

I reside in the United States. In my spare time, I am a voracious reader, foodie, and love to listen to podcasts like This Week In Tech (TWiT), O'Reilly Design Podcast, TED Radio hour, TED Talks, How I built this, e.t.c. I am also a moderate gamer.

Experience

User Experience is something I've always loved. Even though my journey into this field started in 2006, I have been working as a User Experience Design Specialist for about 10 years now, and I have worked with multiple organizations with lots of exciting user centered driven projects. Over the years, I have lead the user exprience design of multiple projects, some of these projects span different sectors like: defense, interactive journalism, corporate hiring and talent acquisition, investment trading portal, UNICEF's RapidSMS Birth Registration; an application that shows national birth registration statistics, election monitoring tools for data capturing deployed multi-nationally, e.t.c.

I believe the user journey is always more rewarding than the destination, so I strive to look for ways to bring awesomeness to the user's fingertips, and also bring on the best user experience design via user centered design into mobile and web applications with my craft. I am a team player and always strive to add value to whatever team or organization I am a part of.
