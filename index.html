Meet Joy

I am a User Experience Design Specialist. I care greatly about users and strive to make day to day impactful user centered driven designs. Design shouldn't just be about slapping a bunch of interfaces together, so I am always looking for ways to bring the best user experience design (a.k.a awesomeness) to user's fingertips by creating aesthetically pleasing simple designs that is easy to learn and use, and meets up to user and system requirements.

I reside in the United States. I am an voracious reader! I am also a foodie, and love to listen podcasts like This Week In Tech (TWiT), O'Reilly Design Podcast, TED Radio hour, TED Talks, How I built this, the list goes on... I am also a moderate gamer, traveler and daredevil explorer.

Experience

User Experience is something I've always loved. I worked 4 years and half years as a User Experience Design Strategist in TimbaObjects Ltd., and worked on lots of exciting user centered driven projects with NGOs like UNICEF and World Bank, I lead the user exprience design of projects like UNICEF's RapidSMS Birth Registration, an application that shows the birth registration statistics across Nigeria, by age range and gender distribution, and other projects like election monitoring tool used to capture data during election monitoring.

As a User Experience Design and User Interface Developer with Irodah Ltd, I worked on some interesting web application projects as well, like ARM Stocktrade, an investment trading portal, and Diakrino, a web portal.

As a Freelance Consultant, I've also worked on some interesting projects, like a human resources recruitment tool for corporate hiring and auditioning, and other projects like an assessment web application tool to help create assessment courses for corporate and individual entities, and so on.

I believe the user journey is always more rewarding than the destination, so I strive to look for ways to bring awesomeness to the user's fingertips, and also bring on the best user experience design into softwares and applications with my craft. I am a team player and always strive to add value to whatever team or organization I am a part of with my skills set.
